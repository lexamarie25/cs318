Blu'$Gold Organization

Blu'$Gold Money Mangagement and Finance Club is a student ran organization through University of Wisconsin- Eau Claire. It is ran by students of all various business background majors. It is a great opportunity of college students to Join!!

You will be involved with many other college students with familair interests as you. It is important to start young with learning how to finance and manage money, so that it can benefit you in the future and get you ahead of the game!
