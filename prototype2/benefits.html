Young Professionals

Blu’$Gold Money management club gives students many opportunities to work with other young professionals and small businesses around the Eau Claire area. Working with these groups make it easy to start networking and making connections that could lead to possible internships and careers!



It is very beneficial to get involved with Blu’$Gold no matter what your major or what year student you are. The possibilites are endless!

Benefits of Blu'$Gold

  1. Helps build your resume that future employers will be looking at

  2. There’s opportunity and room for all different major backgrounds

  3. Networking with companies

  4. Learning how to start budgeting your money for retirement

  5. Working with other UWEC students and peers

  6. Creating great memories with even more great people!

  7. Setting goals and working in a professional envoirnment
